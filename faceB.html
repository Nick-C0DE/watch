Télétravail, cybersécurité : Comment limiter les risques face au COVID-19 ?

"We recorded a 400% increase in phishing attempts during the first week of confinement, ”notes Jérôme Notin, general manager of the cybermalveillance.gouv platform

A recent survey dubbed "Enduring from Home: COVID-19's Impact on Business Security" reveals the increase in cybersecurity incidents since the start of the pandemic and the start of massive teleworking. 200 professionals were surveyed and the results are clear: 24% of the companies surveyed say they have to bear unforeseen costs to deal with cybersecurity incidents.
